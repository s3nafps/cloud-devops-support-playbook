# Outreach Scripts

Use short, specific messages. The offer is not "I do DevOps"; the offer is "I can solve this VPS/Docker/monitoring/backup problem and leave evidence."

## Client Intake Questions

Ask these before accepting the job:

1. What is the app stack?
2. Is it Docker Compose, plain Docker, or something else?
3. What exactly is broken or risky?
4. What result do you need in 48 hours?
5. Is there a recent backup?
6. Can I work through a limited user or platform-approved secure access?
7. Are there production users affected right now?
8. What should not be touched?

## Red Flags

Decline or narrow the scope if:

- the client demands password/private key sharing in chat,
- there is no backup and they want risky production changes,
- the task is actually app development but described as DevOps,
- they expect 24/7 support for a fixed one-time price,
- they want illegal access or bypass work.
